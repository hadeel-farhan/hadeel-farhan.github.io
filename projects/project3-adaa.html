Adaa Website Development

2024

Designed and developed the website for Adaa, a sustainability consultancy, translating UI/UX concepts into a fully functional digital product. Led the design process from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes in Figma and implemented the final site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My Role

  • Led the UI/UX design, creating low- to high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes in Figma.
  • Translated the final designs into a fully functional website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Built a responsive and accessible interface aligned with the company’s sustainability-focused brand.

Impact

  • Created a clear digital presence that communicates Adaa’s sustainability services and expertise to potential clients.
  • Improved information accessibility and service understanding through structured UX and content hierarchy.
  • Enabled easier client outreach and inquiries through intuitive navigation and contact pathways.

Key Skills

UI/UX Design • Wireframing & Prototyping • Front-End Development • HTML • CSS • JavaScript • Responsive Web Design • Product Development
