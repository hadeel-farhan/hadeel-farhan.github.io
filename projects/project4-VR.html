VR EdTech Healthcare Product Design

VR Disaster Triage Simulation

2025



Developed a virtual reality disaster-response simulation that recreates a mass casualty scenario where users must assess and triage patients in a dynamic emergency environment. The experience was later adopted as a lesson template by Zoe Immersive, a global VR education company.

My Role

  • Designed the VR triage simulation experience and interaction flow.
  • Built the simulation using Zoe Immersive, enabling immersive learning scenarios.

Impact

  • Transformed a tabletop disaster-response concept into an immersive training environment that is more accessible.
  • Enabled experiential learning for medical triage decision-making in virtual environments.
  • Selected as a lesson template by Zoe Immersive, supporting future VR educational content development.

Key Skills

Virtual Reality • Interaction Design • Simulation Design • Immersive Learning • Product Prototyping
