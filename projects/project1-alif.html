ALiF - AI Literacy Framework

2025 - Present


Hadeel speaking about AI Literacy at Dubai Health's CEO Townhall


Co-developed one of the Middle East’s first AI Literacy Framework (ALiF) and a corresponding digital learning course to support AI readiness across Dubai Health institutions. The initiative aimed to build foundational understanding of artificial intelligence among healthcare professionals and support the responsible adoption of AI in clinical and operational settings.

My Role

  • Co-developed the AI Literacy Framework, defining core competencies and learning objectives for healthcare professionals.
  • Contributed as a subject matter expert to the design and development of the AI literacy course.
  • Designed interactive learning content using tools such as H5P and Genially to create engaging, accessible learning experiences.
  • Presented the framework and course at WHX Tech Dubai (formally known as Arab Health).
  • Introduced the project at roadshows and the CEO town hall sessions across multiple Dubai Health sites to promote adoption.

First ALiF course: https://learn.mbru.ac.ae/courses/ai-launchpad

Impact

  • Reached 300+ healthcare professionals across multiple sites through internal presentations and institutional roadshows.
  • Delivered an accessible digital learning experience that supports responsible AI awareness and adoption in healthcare.
  • Introduced an AI literacy initiative across Dubai Health institutions, supporting workforce readiness for emerging AI technologies.

Key Skills

Learning Experience Design • Digital Education • AI Literacy • Educational Content Development • H5P • Genially • Public Speaking • Stakeholder Engagement • Healthcare Education
